Frequently Asked Questions (For attendees)
Stay Connected
Maggotty High Alumni Association - US Northern Region (MHAA-USR) is here to help you stay connected to the MHAA-USR and to other alumni. With innovative programs, services, and volunteer opportunities, the MHAA-USR is constantly developing new ways to support and benefit all alumni. Membership is free for life and automatic upon graduation from MHAA-USR or any of its legacy schools.
Our Mission
To strengthen organization, the alumni association builds community and fosters scarlet pride through the meaningful engagement of all current and future alumni.
Get Involved
Connecting with MHAA-USR and with fellow alumni through the MHAA-USR is fun, easy, and rewarding.
  • Special events bring alumni together for sports, travel, entertainment, and family-friendly gatherings.
  • Alumni groups build connections based on interests, school affiliations, or geographic areas.
  • Volunteer opportunities empower alumni to support NexusPeek while making a difference in their communities.
  • Newsletters, social media, and other communication channels keep alumni informed about what is happening at MHAA-USR and across the global alumni community.
