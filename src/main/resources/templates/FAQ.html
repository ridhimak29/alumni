Frequently Asked Questions (For attendees)
Q. What is Maggotty High Alumni Association - US Northern Region (MHAA-USR)?
Maggotty High Alumni Association - US Northern Region (MHAA-USR) is website where alumni can find the events and register for the events. Anyone can easily create an event.
Q. Why does MHAA-USR require sign-up?
In order to register for tickets for events created with MHAA-USR, you need sign-up to MHAA-USR. After completing MHAA-USR sign-up, you can register for the events and pay for events depending upon free or non-free tickets. We ask that you sign-up for an account to view an events of MHAA-USR.
Q. I'm interested in an event but the organizer is a stranger, so I'm wondering if it's really OK for me to attend.
MHAA-USR is unable to judge the contents of an event. Please get in touch with the Event Organizer and make your ticket purchase only when you are sure of the contents of the event.
Q. I want to cancel the ticket I registered, how do I do that?
Attendees cannot cancel their ticket directly. Please contact the event organizer directly to find out if your cancellation can be accepted.
Q. An event for which I had bought a ticket was suddenly canceled. What should I do?
There is a possibility that organizer has deleted the relevant event page. If you're not aware of the reason for the cancellation, please contact the event organizer directly.
